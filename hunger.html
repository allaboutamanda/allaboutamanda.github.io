World Hunger

What is This Issue?

Not every poor person is hungry, but almost all hungry people are poor. The world does not produce enough food to feed everyone. Millions live with hunger and malnourishment because they simply cannot afford to buy enough food, cannot afford nutritious foods or cannot afford the farming supplies they need to grow enough good food of their own.

Why is World Huger a Problelm?

Well first and foremost, The biggest problem with world hunger is the fact that a great number of people are dying. Everyone should have enough food to keep them alive. 11.3% of the world’s population is hungry. That’s roughly 805 million people who go undernourished on a daily basis, consuming less than the recommended 2,100 calories a day.
